Delete Your ADHD FocusKit Account & Data

Effective Date: May 30, 2026
Android

This page explains how to request that your account and associated data be deleted from ADHD FocusKit, developed by YanniDev.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to eching0519@gmail.com from the email address associated with your ADHD FocusKit account.
  2. Include the subject line: "Request to Delete ADHD FocusKit Account".
  3. We will confirm receipt and process your request within 14 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

✉ Email: eching0519@gmail.com

What Data Is Deleted

Upon your request, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your email address, display name, and authentication credentials stored in Firebase Authentication.
  • Subscription records: Your premium subscription status will be revoked. No further charges will occur. Remaining subscription time is non-refundable.

What Data Is Kept

The following data is not accessible to us and is not part of this deletion process:

  • Local app data (tasks, timer history, sound preferences): This data is stored only on your device. It is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app, or you can clear it manually via your device Settings → Apps → ADHD FocusKit → Clear Data.

Additional Retention Period

Account data is permanently deleted from Firebase within 14 business days of receiving your request. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data (e.g., total number of accounts created) may be retained indefinitely, but this data cannot be linked back to you personally.

ADHD FocusKit — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2026
Android

ADHD FocusKit ("we," "our," or "the app") is an ADHD focus and productivity app developed by YanniDev ("the developer"). This policy explains how we handle your information when you use ADHD FocusKit on Android.

1. Information We Collect

ADHD FocusKit is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Account Information: If you create an account (email, Google sign-in), we store your email address and display name through Firebase Authentication.
  • Subscription Data: If you purchase a premium subscription, transaction details are processed by the payment provider. We do not store your full credit card or payment information on our servers.
  • In-App Usage Data: Your tasks, timer sessions, and preferences are stored locally on your device only using on-device storage. This data does not leave your phone.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, track, or share personal health data, ADHD diagnoses, or medical information.
  • We do not use third-party analytics services (no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.).
  • We do not use advertising networks or ad SDKs.
  • We do not track your location, contacts, or device identifiers beyond what is required for app functionality.

3. Device Permissions We Use

ADHD FocusKit requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for Firebase Authentication and subscription management.
  • Modify Audio Settings: Required to play focus soundscapes (brown noise, binaural beats, ambient sounds).
  • Vibrate: Used for haptic feedback on timer events and task completions.
  • Notifications: Used to send optional timer completion reminders (you can disable these in device settings).

4. How We Use Your Information

Your account email is used solely for authentication and subscription verification. Task data, timer history, and sound preferences remain on-device and are never transmitted externally.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All task and preference data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to it.
  • Account authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC), which uses industry-standard encryption.
  • If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data (tasks, settings) is permanently deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC): Handles user sign-in. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: Processes in-app purchases for premium subscriptions. Google Play Terms
  • Expo / EAS (Expo Inc.): Used for app build and deployment infrastructure. Expo Privacy Policy

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

ADHD FocusKit is a general productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Data Retention

  • Local data is retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data via device settings.
  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time (see Section 10).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Effective Date" above and, if required, through in-app notification or email.

10. Your Rights & Contact

You may:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Opt out of notifications via device settings

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: yannidev.contact@gmail.com
